The Asia Property Awards is the region’s biggest and most respected real estate awards programme.

Reflecting the tremendous growth in Asian real estate over the last decade, the Awards originally launched in 2005 in Thailand and now cover eight countries, rewarding only the best developers and developments at a domestic and regional level.

All entries are rigorously and fairly judged by an independent panel of industry experts. All shortlisted companies can therefore be extremely proud of the huge achievement of setting the benchmark for their nation’s real estate sector and, if successful, will go on to represent their country at South East Asia Property Awards grand finale in Singapore and China Property Awards in Hong Kong.
